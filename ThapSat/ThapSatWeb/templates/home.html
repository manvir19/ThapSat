THAPARSAT

ThaparSat is the first Satellite, from the Thapar Satellite Development Centre, which is a Satellite Development Team of the Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology. The NanoSat is planned to be placed in a Sun-Synchronous Polar Orbit and will be particularly used for Multi-Spectral Imaging..MultiSpectral Image Processing consists of 3-10 bands, working in a certain Wavelength Range. Our Satellite operates in a wavelength range of 2-5 micrometers.

The Main applications of the Satellite involve monitoring and visualizing Emission GreenHouse Gases, Estimation of Soil Moisture, and the Estimation of Sea Surface Temperatures. We will be mainly focussing on the Northern India Region.

ThaparSat, is a multi-disciplinary project that started in January,2020. The team handles two facilities, the Satellite Development Lab and the Ground Station. We have students from multiple areas of work, mentored by the faculty heads for each department, working on developing the First NanoSatellite of the Lab.
