The Ground station system will communicate with the satellite by means of Tele Command (TC), Telemetry(TM) and Payload data.It will receive the TM data over the UHF band by using another crossed Yagi antenna and it will down-convert, demodulate, decode and send the data to MCC via Ethernet.It will receive the payload data over the S-Band by using parabolic dish antenna.

The Ground Segment will be used to control and monitor various parameters of the THAPSAT before and after launch. Ground System tracks the satellite and displays the various parameters on the console to monitor the health of the satellite. It displays the payload data to analyze further. It controls the antenna to keep in line with the satellite for better communication.
